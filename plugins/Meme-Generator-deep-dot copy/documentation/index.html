Meme Generator Wordpress Plugin - Unlimit your Wordpress with this Plugin

The Meme Generator Wordpress Plugin is exactly what every Wordpress Blog has been looking for. Everyone loves seeing and making funny memes, so why not let users do that on your website? The Meme Generator Wordpress Plugin has all the features viral meme generator websites have and much more! It let's users see a set group of pictures picked by the admin on the left side and a random bunch of pictures that the admin has approved on the right side of the generator. And if that isn't enough, users can upload their own pictures to make memes with! The Meme Generator Wordpress Plugin let's users download the meme they have generated and also submit it to the website, and if approved by the admin, let it appear on a page on the website. The admin has full control over what memes are published and aren't so that no one can spam your website. This plugin is fun for users and super easy for Wordpress site owners to use!

Install the Plugin

Once you have downloaded the Plugin it's time to install it.

Option 1:

-Log in to your Wordpress Website or Blog and navigate to the plugins tab on the left side. 

-Click Add New

-Select the upload button on the top

-Browse the files on your computer and find  the Meme Generator Wordpress Plugin Zip file and click enter.

-Once uploaded a new screan will appear giving you the option to activate the plugin.

 

Option 2:

You may aupload the files in the Zip manually using an FTP uploader. Ask you hosting provider about this option.

Getting Started

This plugin is very easy to use and only has two short codes.

 

Place the shortcode [nymeme] on the page in your website that you want to make the meme generator page and click publish (or update on an existing page). Once Published you will see the meme generator on that page!

 

This Meme Generator plugin has a great feature that allows users to submit their meme to the website. Once submitted it is up to the admin to allow or delete the meme. Submitted and published memes can be seen on settings page of the meme generator on the right side of the page. (Setting > Meme Generator) 

 

To display the memes you, the admin, approve you have two options. 

 

1. You can create a page and place the shortcode [nymemepub] on it. Then all of the memes you publish from the admin side will show on that page.

 

2. You can also choose not to make a new page. Then when no landing page is mentioned, the plugin will automatically generate a page where all of the publish memes can be viewed on. This page will be linked to from the top left corner of the meme generator page.

 

Note: Pictures should be of at least the width of 400 px.

Note: Sidebars might overlay the meme generator so please hide them on the meme generator page.

Image Control

The Images show on the left and right sides of the meme generator can be controlled by the admin. The plugin comes with several popular memes to get you started. Once on the meme generator settings page you can see which pictures are being shown. You may upload new pictures as well. 

 

On the left side of the Meme Generator there are static main pictures. From the settings page you choose which pictures to make "sticky". These sticky pictures always show up the same way. You may also undo a picture you have made sticky by clicking on "del".

 

On the right side of the Meme generator random meme pictures show up from all of the meme pictures you have allowed to be on the setting page. 


Remember you can upload you own pictures on the meme generator settings page as well! (Note: Pictures should be of at least the width of 400 px.)

 

Users may also upload their own photos to use too!
